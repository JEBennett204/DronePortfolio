About

I’m Jake from Eagle, ID — a cybersecurity graduate and aerial imaging specialist. I blend software engineering, UAS operations, and photogrammetry to deliver production-ready imagery, 3D reconstructions, and web experiences.

I hold a bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity from Boise State University with minors in Japanese Studies and Military Science. My toolkit includes Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and automation for data processing and deployment. I enjoy bridging security-minded engineering with creative aerial applications.
